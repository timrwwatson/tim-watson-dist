About

My name is Tim Watson and I am a Software Engineer.

I graduated with an Msci in Computer Science from Swansea University in 2020. While I was there I took an interest in ray tracing and fractals doing projects in C, C++ and CUDA as well as helping with machine learning lab work in Python. I also got an award for best masters project and best overall performance!

Now I work in security where I’m given lots of interesting problems and enjoy pulling things apart to find solutions. I have also continued mentoring the new graduates and joiners, which was something I really enjoyed at university.

Purpose of this blog

I want to collect the thoughts and steps through programming challenges that I set for myself as well as trying to collate information and create articles that I think might be useful and hopefully interesting too !
